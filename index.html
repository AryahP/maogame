How to Play

Game of Mao: Solo is a puzzle card game where you must discover hidden rules through trial and error.

Objective

Each challenge has a goal: make a certain number of legal plays or empty your hand.

Gameplay

  • Play a Card: Click a card from your hand to attempt to play it on the discard pile
  • Legal Play: If your play follows all hidden rules, it's successful
  • Illegal Play: If you break a rule, you get a penalty and draw a card
  • Draw: Click "Draw Card" if you can't or don't want to play

The Challenge

The rules are hidden. You must learn them by observing which plays succeed and which fail. Pay attention to:

  • Card suits and ranks
  • Card colors (red vs black)
  • Sequences and patterns

Winning

Complete the goal before exceeding the maximum penalties.

Challenge Colors

  • ● Green: Easy puzzles
  • ● Orange: Medium puzzles
  • ● Red: Hard puzzles
